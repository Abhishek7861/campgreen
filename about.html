About Us

An initiavtive to make our villages a better place.

  • Urban tourism is a mainstream travel destination among travel enthusiasts. But rural areas are never in their Expedia list. Rural areas with natural scenic beauty could become a tourist attraction. This will boost the village economy and explorers will have a different terrain to test their feet.
  • Our objective is to discover the rural areas which have the potential to become a tourist attraction. Developing an infrastructure for different adventure activities, which can make it a more exciting destination to take a break from a hectic busy life and spend some quality time with friends and family.
  • These areas are more connected to nature and are more peaceful and less crowded. We aim at making holiday spots relaxing. Tourists can enjoy local traditional cuisines and buy some souvenirs to capture their indelible excursion.
