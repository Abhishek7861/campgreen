Trips

Our startup Camp Green belongs to the domain of the tourism industry. Tourism industry or travel industry is the idea of people travelling to different locations mostly to explore new things or spend time with their families on a holiday or break.

Nawabganj Bird Sanctuary

Unnao district, Kanpur-Lucknow highway, Uttar Pradesh, India

Workshops on wildlife photography, camping, painting and boating will provide an unforgettable experience for our customers. They can be a part of the trip and they can also acquire a skill that they want to learn. This location is at a 1-hour distance from Kanpur and lucknow. Sanctuary has birds, reptiles, deers. A guesthouse with bedding and lodging is provided inside the sanctuary along with a restaurant and a children park. Good opportunity for the individual who needs a break from their regular routine and have a fast-local-trip and learn a new skill too.
